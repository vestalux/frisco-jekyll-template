Rooms

Stylish spaces in an elegant villa.

Superior Salon

Servus 4

The accomodation located in the villa building consists of a large living room and a spacious bedroom. A good night sleep is guaranteed on the king size bed, sofa bed or single bed in the room. The total occupancy for this accomodation is 5. We can provide a baby cot for families staying with us. This room benefits from having a private bathroom attached to it.

Standard Plus

Servus 4

This little room is one of the most booked amoung our facilities. The room located in the villa has three single beds, two of them pushed together by default. The total occupancy is three but we can also provide a baby cot for families staying with us. The room shares the bathroom with one other room.

Standard

Servus 4

The standard room is popular with young couples as it boasts a queen sized double bed. Room is not suitable for extra beds, hence the maximum occupancy is two. The rooms shares the bathroom with one other room.

Double Ensuite

Servus 34

Berek, Balaton and Romantika rooms await our guest with thematic decorations. All rooms have two single beds that are pushed together by default. These rooms generally can't fit an extra bed. The only exception is the Romantika room where can add either an extra bed or a baby cot. The rooms benefit from having their own private bathroom.

Servus 34

The Retro room is ideal for families travelling together. The room has a double bed and on top of it we have a single bunk, making the total occupancy thee. We can also provide a baby cot for the families staying here. The room benefits from having an ensuite private bathroom.
