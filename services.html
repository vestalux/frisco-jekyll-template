Escape room

Price of the service: 20.000 Ft/Game

You are at sea, doing patrol duty, when you see an allied supply ship that you are ordered to sink. After you have done this, a destroyer suddenly appears and in turn attacks you. You need to sink to avoid water bombs, but it's too late. They hit me, the ship was out of control and just sank. You need to repair the machines, start the engines and get up. Watch out, you will soon be discharged 150 meters away and the water pressure will be so high that it will not last more than 60 minutes and may collapse or, in worse cases, run out of breath ... Hurry up, time is running out.
